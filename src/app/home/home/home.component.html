MagnaRisa, the best friends you'll ever have.

Originally founded around a survival Minecraft server back in 2010, this community is all about gaming together and forming friendships that last a lifetime. Some of the games we play besides Minecraft are Among Us, Hypixel, Fall Guys, Deep Rock Galactic, Fortnite, and various browser games. MagnaRisa loosely translates to large laugh and there's no shortage of those around here. The community is built around the pillars of inclusion, family-friendliness, respect for others, and striving to build each other up to be better.

Join our Discord to hang out with everyone and craft some friendships.
