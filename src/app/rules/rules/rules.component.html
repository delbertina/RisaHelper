Ban Appeal

So you want to appeal your ban eh?
Well you're off to a good start, you have enough brains to find this while reading our rules.
Now read the following very carefully.

Before submitting an appeal, please consider the following.

The appeal process is the only way to be unbanned
Circumventing your ban may bar you from ever being unbanned
You may only submit an appeal one time
Take your time contemplating what you want to write
Consider your appeal rejected until you hear otherwise
If you leave discord we will not continue considering your appeal
Breaking our discord rules may earn you a ban from this discord too
We review and re-review appeals at staff meetings
Staff meetings typically occur every 1-3 months
Pestering anyone about your application will negatively affect your chances

The appeal form link is bit.ly/MagnaRisaBanAppeal####

Each # corresponds to the number of a specific rule.
First Number = The rule about English only
Second Number = The rule about griefing
Third Number = The rule about spamming
Fourth Number = The rule about hacking
